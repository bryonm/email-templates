Dear Friend,

Exchanging Christmas gifts with family and friends is a blessed time because it reminds us of Christ’s birth — God’s miraculous gift to us!

Celebrate that glorious gift even more profoundly this year. Open your heart and Be Someone’s Miracle by blessing the poor in developing countries through the Cross Catholic Outreach Christmas Catalog.

Want to help a family put a real roof over their heads? Or make it possible for them to have enough to eat for a month? Or send kids reading glasses for school or a soccer ball to play with?

Your gift will send these important items to impoverished families around of the world.

Here’s how it works:
  1. Choose from a variety of meaningful gifts. You can find one that’s close to your heart and fits your budget.
  2. Select the amount you wish to donate. We set up the catalog so that any amount you give will make an impact.
  3. Finish in moments with our secure and easy checkout. And just like that, you’ll bring long-term blessings to a family who desperately needs them.
The needs are many, but so are the ways you can give! There are over 30 gifts in this year’s catalog, including:
Microloans
Roofing Materials
Basic Medicine
Blankets
By giving a gift from our Christmas Catalog, you have the opportunity to share your faith in Christ and honor a loved one while blessing children and families in a developing country.
